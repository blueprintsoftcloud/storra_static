Complete Commerce System

Everything needed to run, track and grow a store.

Storra has two connected sides: a smooth customer experience for shopping and ordering, and a strong admin experience for managing products, orders, payments, staff and store operations.

01

Product & Category Management

Create product categories, add product details, upload images, update pricing and organize your store structure clearly.

02

Customer Ordering Portal
